With one day to do something big for the three of us, Steve and Ever
opted to climb Thompson Peak, partly because of my urgent recommendation!
I thought they'd enjoy it a lot. Ever also likes to climb highest summits
of an area, and this is the highest peak of the Sawtooths so there's that!

The drive is long, but the hiking to high alpine country not especially
exhausting. Then, the cross country travel above the lake is
continually interesting, though the final boulderfield can feel tedious.
