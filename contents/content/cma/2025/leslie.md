Steve introduced me to the interesting Leslie Gulch in remote eastern
Oregon. Fascinating towers rising from a desert landscape. Very nice!
We hiked up a gully and looked down on a canyon and lake from a summit
called the Yellow Jacket. We investigated
possible rock climbs, though rock quality appeared to be quite
variable.
