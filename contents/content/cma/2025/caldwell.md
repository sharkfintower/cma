I walked from Steve's place south into Caldwell and thence down to the
Deer Flat National Wildlife Refuge. It was a bit under 14 miles.
I started a bit before dawn and enjoyed seeing the sun rise.
Downtown Caldwell is really nice and it was fun walking through some older
neighborhoods. A lot of people have RVs or campers.

Later, I climbed a hill and reached more barren country, though ingeniously
irrigated.

Steve picked me up at the Refuge, thanks much!
