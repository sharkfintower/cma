Robert and I made the very short hike to some ice climbs in the
Amphitheater area of Hyalite. Robert led three pitches, and I top-roped
all of them. First time on ice in many years...it's taking time but I hope
to become fluent again!

* Switchback Falls CWI3)
* Snoochie (WI4)
* Thin Chance (WI4-)

Snoochie looked quite thin, yellow ice over black rock, but it didn't feel
that difficult. I had a harder time on Switchback Falls, hammering away at
placements and knocking off dinner plates!

Thin Chance was fun, over in an area with many other folks nearby.

I didn't have any ice clippers on my harness, so racking a screw was really tough.
I pretty much had to use two hands...
