Not used to steep Alps, I took the lift up to hike the Zielspitze.
A great hike, listening to music all the while, I reached the summit and
enjoyed a look around. There was only a bit of snow on north-facing slopes
below the ridge crest where the route climbs the summit block.

{% image /cmaimages/2025/3_IMG_6121.jpg %}

I briefly thought about going down another way to get back into the Texel
Mountains to say goodbye to them, but I contented myself with an ordinary
descent.

Drove back to Markt Schwaben, ordered a pizza and hid in the hostile house
I almost called home. Bullets have been dodged.
